Web of Science Category Examples

  • WC=Agricultural Engineering retrieves records in which this term appears in the Web of Science Category field of a Full Record.

  • WC=(Biochemical Research Methods AND Biochemistry & Molecular Biology) retrieves records in which both terms must appear in the Web of Science Category field on a Full Record.

  • WC=(Cell Biology OR Cell & Tissue Engineering) retrieves records in which either (or both) terms appear in the Web of Science Category field on a Full Record.

  • WC=(Cell Biology NOT Cell & Tissue Engineering) retrieves records in which the term Cell Biology appears in the Web of Science Category field on a Full Record, but the term Cell & Tissue Engineering cannot appear in that same field.

Did You Know ...

Web of Science Category terms are traditional terms assigned to a record by Thomson Reuters.

Category terms are journal-based. On the Full Record page, the Web of Science Category field displays the traditional terms assigned to a record.
